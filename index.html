A Place for Dogs
A Place for Dogs provides high quality services for both you and your pooch! From daycare to training, our team of educated animal lovers strives to take care of your furry friend as if they were our own. With us, your dog is family.
What We Offer
Daycare
Our daycare facility is full of enrichment activities for your dog to enjoy! No matter how long their stay is, we guarantee they'll have a paw-some day.
Boarding
Does your dog need a place to stay? We got you covered. With extra padded dog beds, blankets, and heated flooring, your dog will feel safe and cozy every night.
Grooming
We also offer grooming services! From nail trims to hair cuts, our certified groomers are ready to get your dog dolled up for their next selfie.
Training
Positive reinforcement is our top priority when it comes to training. Whether it's group or private lessons, we work with your dog to set them up for success. Come schedule a meet and greet to learn more!
